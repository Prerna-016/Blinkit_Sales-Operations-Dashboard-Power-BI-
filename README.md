# 📊 Blinkit Sales & Operations Dashboard (Power BI)

## 📌 Overview

This project presents an interactive **Power BI dashboard** built to analyze the sales, customer behavior, and operational performance of Blinkit (online grocery delivery platform). The dashboard provides actionable insights to help understand business trends and improve decision-making.

---

## 🎯 Objectives

* Analyze overall sales performance
* Understand customer purchasing patterns
* Identify top-performing products and categories
* Track outlet performance across locations
* Provide data-driven insights for business growth

---

## 📂 Dataset Description

The dataset used in this project includes:

* Product details (category, type, pricing)
* Outlet information (location, size, type)
* Sales metrics (revenue, quantity sold)
* Customer ratings and feedback

---

## 📊 Key Features of Dashboard

* **Total Sales Overview** – Displays overall revenue and performance trends
* **Outlet Analysis** – Comparison based on outlet size, type, and location
* **Product Category Insights** – Identifies best and worst performing categories
* **Customer Preferences** – Analysis based on ratings and buying patterns
* **Year-wise Trends** – Growth and performance over time

---

## 🔍 Key Insights

* High-performing categories contribute significantly to total revenue
* Certain outlet sizes and locations generate more sales
* Customer ratings influence product demand
* Sales trends show growth patterns over time
